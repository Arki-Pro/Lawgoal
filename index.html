Why Legoal?

Law aspirants face scattered resources across PDFs, Telegram groups, and coaching sites. Generic apps provide irrelevant content, wasting study time.

Legoal centralizes PYQs, mock tests, notes, and daily updates in one clean interface with AI recommendations. Focused. Efficient. Personalized.

Organized PYQs

Smart Analytics

Track high-yield topics and aspirant progress with AI insights.

Distraction-Free

Clean, ad-free interface for uninterrupted learning.
