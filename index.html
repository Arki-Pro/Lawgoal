The Aspirant's Challenge

Law aspirants often face significant hurdles. Valuable Previous Year Questions (PYQs) are scattered across countless PDFs, Telegram groups, and coaching sites. This fragmentation forces students to waste precious time searching for materials instead of focusing on their studies. Most generic prep apps fail to provide law-specific content, leaving students with irrelevant information and an inefficient study experience. Legoal addresses these pain points directly.

Introducing Legoal: Your AI-Powered Legal Guide

Legoal is the first centralized platform designed exclusively for Judiciary, AIBE, Law Officer, and Clerkship exams in India. Our AI-powered mobile application offers structured syllabus, daily law updates, comprehensive notes, and interactive flashcards. With a clean, ad-free interface and an affordable subscription, Legoal transforms your study experience, making it efficient, focused, and personalized.
